About Bells

In the Animal Crossing universe, Bells are the main currency. Players earn them by collecting fruits, catching bugs, fishing, and selling items. In the crypto world, we mine them instead.

Bells uses random block rewards to mimic the unpredictable nature of the in-game economy.
